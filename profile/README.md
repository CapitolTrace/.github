### 🔍 What we build

Capitol Trace aggregates **25+ public government data sources** into connected, searchable intelligence that any citizen, journalist, or researcher can use — **completely free**. We track the current congressional roster — how they vote, who funds them, what they trade, who lobbies them — and surface patterns that no single database reveals.

> *We don't tell you who to vote for. We show you the record. You decide what it means.*
